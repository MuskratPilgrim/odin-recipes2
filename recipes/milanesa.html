Milanesa de Carne

This is my favorite dish of all time. OF ALL TIME!

Ingredients

  • bread crumbs
  • egg
  • flour
  • beef
  • marinade

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef.
  2. Beat some eggs. They had it coming.
  3. Dip the meat in flour.
  4. Dip the floured meat in the humiliated eggs.
  5. Dip the floured-egged meat in the bread crumbs.
  6. Fry for like 5 minutes.
  7. Om nom nom.
